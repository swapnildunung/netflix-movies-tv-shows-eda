# 🎬 Exploratory Data Analysis of Netflix Movies & TV Shows

An end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project using Python to analyze Netflix's global content library, uncovering trends in content production, genres, countries, audience ratings and release patterns to support data-driven content strategy.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix Movies and TV Shows using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib and Seaborn. The analysis focuses on understanding content distribution, geographic production, audience targeting, release trends and content evolution over time through exploratory data analysis and feature engineering.

---

## Business Problem

A streaming platform wants to understand its global content portfolio, identify audience preferences, evaluate regional content distribution and discover opportunities to optimize future content acquisition and production strategies.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze global content distribution across countries.
- Compare Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify release trends across decades and months.
- Evaluate audience rating distribution.
- Discover top content-producing directors.
- Generate business insights for content strategy.

---

## Dataset

- Domain: Entertainment
- Records: 8,807 Netflix titles
- Features: 17
- Includes:
  - Movies & TV Shows
  - Countries
  - Directors
  - Ratings
  - Genres
  - Release Year
  - Date Added
  - Duration

---

## Data Preparation

- Cleaned missing and inconsistent values.
- Corrected rating and duration anomalies.
- Converted date fields into datetime format.
- Created engineered features including decade, month, year and audience rating buckets.

## Key Business Insights

- United States contributes the largest share of Netflix content, followed by India and the United Kingdom.
- Movies dominate the content library, while TV Shows have grown significantly in recent years.
- Content production increased rapidly after 2010, reflecting Netflix's global expansion.
- Adult and Teen categories account for the majority of available content, indicating a mature audience focus.
- Release activity remains relatively consistent throughout the year with slight seasonal variations.
- A small group of directors contributes a significant portion of Netflix's catalog.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Expand investment in high-growth international markets beyond North America.
- Increase production of successful TV Show formats to capitalize on growing viewer demand.
- Strengthen regional content strategies based on country-level production trends.
- Optimize content acquisition using audience rating preferences.
- Continue leveraging data-driven insights for content planning and platform growth.
